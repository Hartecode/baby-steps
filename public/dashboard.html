About

Baby Steps is a great app to keep track of your baby’s milestones. You can track each milestone, and the date on which it happened. Babies grow up fast, but Baby Steps helps you document every first along the way!


This app has been designed and coded by Sean Harte.


Get Started!

You currently have no baby added. Click on “Add baby” to enter your baby’s information. Once added, you can click on the baby’s name under “add new milestone” or click on the snapshot of the baby to go to milestone page.
